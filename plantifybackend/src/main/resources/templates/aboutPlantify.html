About Plantify

Creating Plantify was a long and not-easy journey. We spent almost a day brainstorming ideas for our project, then we thought "What would be a good way to improve our day-to-day lives as busy students?" and collectively agreed that a little greenery would make us more motivated and satisfied with our surroundings. And, because taking care of a bunch of plants would be too time-consuming and meticulous, therefore we decided that our product would revolve around solving this issue.

After coming up with the idea the hard part came: building a functioning product and a user-friendly platform that manages the plant's details. The product itself is made using Arduino products and coded in C language.

Then, we incorporated all the physical elements into a custom 3D-printed pot that was made in collaboration with Antistatic. Everything is concealed by the pot and the plywood surrounding it so that the inside wires are not damaged. More, inside there is a plastic pump that takes the optimal quantity of water necessary that is stored in a reservoir at the bottom of the product.
Around the pot, there is a LED strip that indicates the status of the plant: green means the plant is between optimal parameters, yellow that one or two parameters are out of bound, and red indicates that the plant is in critical condition.
